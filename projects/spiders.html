Spiders
Project Description
A 3D Creature made in Zbrush, Blender, and Substance Painter. The spiders were made with intentions of including them as an enemy for a small game project. In addition to a black widow, a wolf spider(which has its high poly sculpt featured in a stylized render above) was also designed.
